Description

The Intel Core i7-10870H is a central processing unit (CPU) developed by Intel and released in Q4 2020. It is part of the 10th Generation Intel Core processor family and is based on Intel's 14nm Comet Lake architecture.

The i7-10870H has eight cores and sixteen threads, with a base clock speed of 2.2 GHz and a maximum turbo frequency of 5.0 GHz. It has 16 MB of Intel Smart Cache and a thermal design power (TDP) of 45 watts.

The i7-10870H supports dual-channel DDR4 memory with a maximum speed of 2933 MHz and has Intel UHD Graphics 630 with a maximum dynamic frequency of 1.25 GHz. It also has discrete graphics support for higher performance graphics cards.

The i7-10870H is designed for use in high-performance laptops that require significant processing power. It supports Intel Turbo Boost Technology 2.0 and Intel Hyper-Threading Technology for improved performance in multi-tasking scenarios. It also supports Intel vPro technology for enhanced security and manageability.

Overall, the i7-10870H is a powerful processor that can handle demanding applications such as gaming, video editing, and content creation. Its high core count and clock speeds make it a good choice for users who need a lot of processing power in a laptop form factor.
