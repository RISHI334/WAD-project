Description

The Intel Core i5-12500H is a central processing unit (CPU) developed by Intel and released in Q3 2022. It is part of the 12th Generation Intel Core processor family and is based on Intel's 10nm SuperFin process technology.

The i5-12500H has six cores and twelve threads, with a base clock speed of 2.4 GHz and a maximum turbo frequency of 4.6 GHz. It has 12 MB of Intel Smart Cache and a thermal design power (TDP) of 35 watts.

The i5-12500H supports dual-channel DDR4 memory with a maximum speed of 3200 MHz and has Intel UHD Graphics with a maximum dynamic frequency of 1450 MHz. It also has discrete graphics support for higher performance graphics cards.

The i5-12500H is designed for use in high-performance laptops that require significant processing power. It supports Intel Turbo Boost Max Technology 3.0 and Intel Hyper-Threading Technology for improved performance in multi-tasking scenarios. It also supports Intel vPro technology for enhanced security and manageability.

Overall, the i5-12500H is a powerful processor that can handle demanding applications such as gaming, video editing, and content creation. Its high core count and clock speeds make it a good choice for users who need a lot of processing power in a laptop form factor. Additionally, the i5-12500H features Intel's latest CPU architecture and process technology, providing improved performance and power efficiency compared to previous generations of Intel processors.
