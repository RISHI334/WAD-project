Description

The i5-10500 has six cores and twelve threads, with a base clock speed of 3.1 GHz and a maximum turbo frequency of 4.5 GHz. It has 12 MB of Intel Smart Cache and a thermal design power (TDP) of 65 watts.

The i5-10500 supports dual-channel DDR4 memory with a maximum speed of 2666 MHz and has Intel UHD Graphics 630 with a maximum dynamic frequency of 1.2 GHz.

The i5-10500 is compatible with Intel's LGA 1200 socket and is designed for use in desktop systems that require a mid-range CPU. It supports Intel Virtualization Technology (VT-x) and Intel Hyper-Threading Technology for improved performance in multi-tasking and virtualization scenarios.

Overall, the i5-10500 is a capable processor for applications such as gaming, office productivity, and light content creation. Its six cores and high clock speeds make it a good choice for demanding applications that require significant processing power, but it is also efficient enough to be used in more power-conscious environments.
