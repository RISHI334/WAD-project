About this product

Features:

The Kingston 480GB A400 SATA SSD is a solid-state drive designed for use in a range of applications, from desktop and laptop computers to gaming systems and servers. Some of the key features of the Kingston 480GB A400 SATA SSD include:

High-speed performance: The Kingston 480GB A400 SATA SSD uses the latest 3D NAND technology and is equipped with a SATA III interface, which enables it to deliver fast read and write speeds of up to 500 MB/s and 450 MB/s, respectively.

Large storage capacity: The Kingston 480GB A400 SATA SSD provides a storage capacity of 480GB, which makes it suitable for storing large amounts of data, including music, videos, and high-resolution images.

Enhanced reliability: The Kingston 480GB A400 SATA SSD features a rugged design that is resistant to shock and vibration, which helps to ensure data integrity and prevent data loss.

Advanced data encryption: The Kingston 480GB A400 SATA SSD supports hardware-based AES 256-bit encryption, which provides advanced security features and ensures that sensitive data remains protected.

Long lifespan: The Kingston 480GB A400 SATA SSD is designed to provide high endurance and reliability, with a mean time between failures (MTBF) of up to 1 million hours.

Improved software: The Kingston 480GB A400 SATA SSD comes with the Kingston SSD Manager software, which allows users to manage and optimize their drive's performance, monitor the health of the drive, and enable firmware updates.
