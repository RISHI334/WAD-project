About this product

Features

The Samsung 870 EVO SATA SSD is a high-performance solid-state drive that offers a range of features designed to optimize its speed, reliability, and endurance. Some of the key features of the Samsung 870 EVO SATA SSD include:

High-speed performance: The Samsung 870 EVO SATA SSD uses the latest V-NAND technology and is equipped with a SATA III interface, which enables it to deliver fast read and write speeds of up to 560 MB/s and 530 MB/s, respectively.

Large storage capacity: The Samsung 870 EVO SATA SSD is available in a range of storage capacities, ranging from 250 GB to 4 TB, which makes it suitable for a wide range of applications.

Enhanced reliability: The Samsung 870 EVO SATA SSD features a robust design that is resistant to shock, vibration, and temperature extremes. It also includes advanced error correction and detection technologies to ensure data integrity and prevent data loss.

Advanced data encryption: The Samsung 870 EVO SATA SSD supports hardware-based AES 256-bit encryption, which provides advanced security features and ensures that sensitive data remains protected.

Long lifespan: The Samsung 870 EVO SATA SSD is designed to provide high endurance and reliability, with a mean time between failures (MTBF) of up to 1.5 million hours.

Improved software: The Samsung 870 EVO SATA SSD comes with Samsung's Magician software, which allows users to manage and optimize their drive's performance, monitor the health of the drive, and enable firmware updates.
